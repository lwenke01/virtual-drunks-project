Rules of High and Low

Guess wether the next card will be high or low

  • First, you'll be shown a card.
  • Then, all you have to do is guess wether the next card shown will be high or low.
  • If you are wrong, you'll have to take a drink.
  • If the player guesses right, your opponent will have to take a drink
  • The game will continue until we have gone through the entire deck.
